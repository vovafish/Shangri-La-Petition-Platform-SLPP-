About Shangri-La Petition Platform (SLPP)

The Shangri-La Petition Platform empowers citizens to voice their opinions and bring important issues to the attention of the Shangri-La Parliament. Through this platform, citizens can propose petitions, support causes they believe in, and drive meaningful change in their community.

Our Mission:

To create a transparent, secure, and accessible platform for civic engagement and influence parliamentary decisions.

How It Works:

  1. Citizens create petitions on issues they care about.
  2. Other citizens sign petitions to show their support.
  3. Once a petition reaches the required signature threshold, it is debated in Parliament.
  4. The Petitions Committee responds with a summary after the debate, and the petition is formally closed.

Key Features:

  • Open access to petition data and statistics for the public, media, and organizations.
  • Secure and verified signatures to ensure authenticity.
  • A transparent process for parliamentary debate and response.
